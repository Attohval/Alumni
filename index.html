About Our Alumni

DCCW ALUMNI 2000 Association is a vibrant network of graduates, united by shared experiences and a passion for lifelong learning, leadership, and service. We celebrate our diverse journeys and empower each other to make a difference in our communities and professions.

Our Mission

To connect, inspire, and support alumni in their personal and professional growth, while giving back to our alma mater and society.

Core Values

  • Mentorship & Collaboration
  • Celebrating Achievements
  • Community Service
  • Inclusivity & Diversity

Our Story

Founded in 1992, our alumni community has grown to thousands of members worldwide, united by a commitment to excellence and connection.
