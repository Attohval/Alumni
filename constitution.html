Preamble

We, the alumni of Vtech, united by shared experiences and a commitment to lifelong learning, establish this constitution to guide our association, foster community, and promote excellence.

Article I: Name & Purpose

  • Name: The organization shall be known as the Vtech Alumni Association.
  • Purpose: To connect alumni, support professional and personal growth, and give back to our alma mater and society.

Article II: Membership

  • Membership is open to all graduates and former students of Vtech.
  • Members shall uphold the values and mission of the association.
  • Membership benefits include networking, mentorship, and event participation.

Article III: Leadership

  • The association shall be governed by elected officers: President, Vice President, Secretary, Treasurer, and other roles as needed.
  • Officers shall serve for a term of two years and may be re-elected.
  • Duties include organizing events, managing finances, and representing alumni interests.

Article IV: Meetings

  • General meetings shall be held at least once per year.
  • Special meetings may be called by the President or a majority of officers.
  • Quorum for meetings shall be 20% of active members.

Article V: Amendments

  • This constitution may be amended by a two-thirds vote of active members present at a general meeting.
  • Proposed amendments must be submitted in writing at least two weeks prior to the meeting.
